Getting started with R

Date: 21 August 2020 2:00pm–3:30pm (Online seminar)

Speaker: Martin Schweinberger, School of Languages and Cultures, UQ

Abstract: Looking for ways to automate some of those repetitive data analysis tasks? This introductory session will give you an idea of what you can do with R and whether you would like to use it for your data analysis. Please install R and R Studio prior(!) to the workshop. Instructions from the UQ Library regarding installing R and R Studio can be found here. Once R Studio is installed you will need to install the tidyverse package by opening R Studio and typing at the prompt in the console the following command:

install.packages("tidyverse")

Please refer to this short video from LinkedIn Learning for full instructions. (Please use your UQ email address and then login using Single Sign On). The Research Seminars will now be held online via Zoom, available only to University of Queensland staff and students.
