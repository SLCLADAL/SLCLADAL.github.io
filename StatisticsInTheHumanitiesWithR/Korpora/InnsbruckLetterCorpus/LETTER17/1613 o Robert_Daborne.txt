Sr I hav bin twise to speak with you both for the sheet I told you off as also to know yr determination for the company whea_ther you purpose they shall have the play or noe They rale upon me I hear bycause the kingsmen hav given out they shall hav it if you please I will make you full amends for thear wrong to you in my last play before they get this for I know it is this play must doe them good if you purpose any to them I hav sent you 2 sheets more so y t you hav x sheets and I desyre you to send me 30s more which is just eight pound besyds my rent which I will fully satisfy you eather by them or the king's men as you please Good s r let me know yr mynd for I desyre to make you part of amends for yr great fryndship to me wishing my labor or service could deserv you so trusting one yr gen_tlenes which cannot long be without satisfaction now I rest ever at yr commaund ROB DABORNE Lent M rs Daborne upon this bille more the 29 of october 1613 xxs
