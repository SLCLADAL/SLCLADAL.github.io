Mr Hinchlow my trubles drawing to some end have forced me to be trublesom to you beyond my purpose bycause I would be free at any rate some papers I have sent you though not so fayr written all as I could wish I will now wholy intend to finishe my promise which though it come not within compass of this Term shall come upon ye neck of this new play they ar now studyinge my request is the xl might be made up whear of I have had 9l if you please to appoynt any houer to read to M r Allin I will not fayle nor after this day loose any time till it be concluded My best rememberance to you I rest yo rs ROBT DABORNE 8 May 1613
