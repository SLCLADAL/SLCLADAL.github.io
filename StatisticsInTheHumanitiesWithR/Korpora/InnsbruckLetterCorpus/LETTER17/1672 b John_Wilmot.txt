From our Tubb att M ns Fourcards this 18nt of Octob Wife Our gutt has allready binn griped & wee are now in bed soe that wee are not in a condition of writing either according to thy merritt or our desert wee therefore doe command thy benigne acceptance of these our letters in what way soever by us inscribed or directed willing thee therewithall to assure our sole daughter & heire issue female the Lady ann part of our best respects this with your care & dilligence in the erection of our furnaces is att present the utmost of our will & pleasure
