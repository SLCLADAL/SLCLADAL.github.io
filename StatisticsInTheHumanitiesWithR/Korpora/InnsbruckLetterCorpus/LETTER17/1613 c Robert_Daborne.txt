M Hinchlow the company told me you wear expected thear yesterday to conclude about thear comming over or goinge to Oxford I have not only labord my own play which shall be ready before they come over but given Cyrill Tourneur an act of ye Arreignment of London to write y t we may have y t like_wise ready for them I wish you had spoken with them to know thear resolution for they depend upon yr purpose I hav sent you 2 sheets more fayr written upon my ffayth s r they shall not stay one howr for me whearfor I beseech you as heatherto so you would now spare me 40s which stands me upon to send over to my counsell in a matter concerns my whole estate and wher I deale otherways then to yr content may I and myne want ffryndship in distress! so relying one yr favor which shall never reap loss by me I rest at yr commaund ROB DABORNE 5 0 June 1613 Receved by me Garred Leniaghe xxs
