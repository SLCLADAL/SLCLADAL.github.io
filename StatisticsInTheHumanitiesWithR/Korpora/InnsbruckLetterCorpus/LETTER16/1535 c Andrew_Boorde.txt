After humly salutacyon Acordyng to my dewte coactyd I am (causeys consideryd) to geue to yow notycyon of certyn synystrall matters contrary to our realme of ynglond specyally agenst our most armipotentt perpondentt cricumspecte dyscrete and gracyose soue reyng lord the Kynge for sens my departyng from yow I haue per lustratyd normandy frawnce gascony and Byon èe regyons also of castyle byscay spayne paarte of portyngale and returned thorow Arogon Nauerne and now am att burdyose In the whych partyes I hard of dyuerse credyble persons of èe sayd countryes and also of rome ytale and almen èat the pope èe emprowre and all other crystyn kyngis with èer peple (èe french kyng except) be sett agenst our souereyne lord èe kynge apon the which in all the nacyons èat I haue trauellyd a greatt army and navey ys preparyd and few frendys ynglond hath in theys partes of Europe as Iesus your louer knowyth who euer haue your master and yow with èe hole realme vnder hys vynges of tuyssyon! from burdyose the xx day of Iune by èe hond of your saruantt and bedman Andrew Boord I humyly and precordyally desyre your mastershepp to be good master (as yow euer haue byn) to your faythfull bedmen master prior of the cherter howse of london and to Master docter Horde prior of Hynton directed on back To hys vererable master Master Thomas Cromwell secretory to our souereyngne lord th kyng be èis byll directyd
