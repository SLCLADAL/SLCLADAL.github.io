Cossin Plompton after my hartie recommendations your servant sheweth me ye were and have bene very sick wherof I am very sory And if your sicknes continue wheare the commaundment comes forth send forth your servants and tennants and send forth your excuse to my Lord Lieutennant with a letter of the trough of your sicknes and of the time of the continuance therof and being advertised of the truth he will excep of your lawfull and reasonable excuse And thus hertely far you well From York the xxviij of May Anno 1545 37 H 8 Your asurred kinsman Henry Savill 27 May 1545
