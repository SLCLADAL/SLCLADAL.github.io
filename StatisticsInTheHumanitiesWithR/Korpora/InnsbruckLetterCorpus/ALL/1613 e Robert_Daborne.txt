M r Hinchlow I am inforced to make bold with you for one 20s more of ye xl and one Fryday night I will deliver in ye 3 acts fayr written and then receav ye other 40s and if you please to have some papers now you shall but my promise shall be as good as bond to you and if you will let me have perusall of any other book of y rs I will after Fryday intend it speedyly and doubt not to giv you full content so with my best remem_branc I rest at yr commaund ROBT DABORNE 3 May 1613
