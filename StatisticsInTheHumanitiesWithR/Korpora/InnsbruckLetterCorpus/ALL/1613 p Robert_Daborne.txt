Sr yr man was with me whoe found me wrighting the last scean which I had thought to have brought you to night but it will be late ear I can doe it and being satterday night my oc_cation urges me to request you spare me xs more and for yr mony if you please not to stay till Johnson's play be playd the king's men hav bin very earnest with me to pay you in yr mony for yr curtesy whearin you shall have 30s proffit with many thanks Purposing to morow night if you call not upon me to com and shew you fynis I pray Sr supply this my last occa_tion which crowns ye rest of yr curtesies to which I will now giv speedy requitall resting ever at yr commaund ROB DABORNE Nov 13 1613
