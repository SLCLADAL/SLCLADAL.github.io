Good Mr Hinchlow I am upon ye sodeyn put to great ex_tremyty in bayling my man comitted to Newgate upon taking a possession for me and I took less mony of my kinsman a lawier y t was with me then servd my turn I am thearfor to beseech you to spare me xxs which will doe me so great plea_sure y t you shall find me thankfull and performing more then ever I promisd or am tyed to so bold upon so great an occation to truble you I crave yr favorable interpretation and rest ever at yr comaund ROB DABORNE 28 Aprill 1613 Lente M r Daborne this money witness HUGH ATWELL
