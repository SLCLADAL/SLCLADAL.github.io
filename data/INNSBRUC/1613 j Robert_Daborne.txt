Mr Hinchlow of all ffryndship let me beholding to you for one xxs which shall be the last I will request till the play be fully by us ended Upon my honnest ffayth with you which I will never break I will request no more and soe much will be due to me then Sr this is my last request of yr trouble which my speedy occation presses me to soe I rely upon yr lov hearin for which you shall ever command me ROB DABORNE 16 July 1613 dd this xxs the 16 th July 1613
