The alteration of my mothers former resolutions (who is now resolv'd against ever moving from hence) puts mee upon some thoughts w ch were allmost quite out of my head but you may be sure I shall determine nothing that does not tend as much to your reall happiness as lies in my power I have there_fore sent you this letter to prepare you for a remove first hither & afterwards as fate shall direct which is (I find) the true disposer of things whatever wee attribute to wisdome or providence bee therefore in a readiness upon the first notice from mee to put that in execution w ch I shall first informe you particularly of let me have an answar & dispatch this messenger quickly God bless you yours Rochester These for the C of R
